Cold Brew Coffee

Directions

1) Combine

Mix 1 cup coarse-ground coffee with 4 cups filtered water in a jar or French press. Stir to wet all grounds.

2) Steep

Cover and refrigerate 12–18 hours. Longer = stronger. Keep it simple and let time do the work.

3) Strain

Pour slowly through a fine filter or cheesecloth. Repeat for extra clarity.

4) Dilute

Start 1:1 with cold water or milk over ice. Tweak to taste.

5) Store

Keep sealed in the fridge for up to 7 days. Label your batch date.

Pro Tips

Try a pinch of salt, a dash of simple syrup, or cinnamon. Lighter roasts = brighter, dark roasts = chocolaty.
